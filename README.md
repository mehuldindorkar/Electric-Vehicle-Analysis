To understand more details is given below:- 

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze 150K+ electric vehicle registrations.

Performed data cleaning, transformation, and modeling using Power Query for accuracy.

Used DAX calculations to build reliable metrics and measure key performance trends.

Identified that 78% of all registrations were Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs).

Found that Tesla dominated the market with ~69K registrations, representing about 46% market share.

Designed time-series visualizations to show strong EV adoption growth from 2010 to 2023.

Added regional map visuals to highlight state-wise and area-wise registration patterns.

Included slicers for flexible filtering and deeper drill-downs by model, year, and region.

The dashboard helps users clearly understand market trends, brand dominance, and EV growth patterns over time.
